How Does Camel Wool work?

Camel wool fibers are not straight but are bent forming crimps. The mechanical wool action on the body has a micro-massaging effect on the body which helps blood supply to the organ.
The springiness, resiliency, thermal conductivity of camel wool makes it very favorable for therapeutic use, Camel wool products are breathable, exchanging air between body part and its surroundings. Camel wool also has thermostatic properties which protects and insulates in cold weather as well as keep is cool in warmer temperature. “For example, peripheral vascular disease associated with metabolic disease ( i.e. Diabetes’s, arteriosus ) arthritis, paresis and paralysis of peripheral nervous all require intervention of yielding heat therapy. Wool products such as garments linings shoulder straps, knee pads and lumber pads exert a very positive effect on the above mentioned circulatory disturbances.

Manufacturing in Mongolia

Mongolia’s Gobi desert is a mysterious place of untouched beauty and hidden treasures. Picturesque mountains, gushing rivers, sandy dunes and wide open country is the heart of the camel land.
Extremely cold winters followed by intensely hot summers are characterized by the landscape and its dwellers.
The Bactrian camel is perfectly equipped for such harsh climate. The soft luxurious hair of the camel has been used for therapeutic clothing and household use for thousands of years.
100% designed in Canada, made in Mongolia.
All Camel Heal line of products are manufactured by our sister company the Mongolia Heal Company in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia.
Ever since our beginning in 2013, we have searched for quality Camel Wool Products, in 2018 we were able to register a manufacturing company with Mongolia colleges to produce and export Camel Heal Line of quality luxury therapeutic products.
The wool used in clothing is mainly camel hair and Yak wool from the Gobi Desert in Mongolia, the wool is spun and dyed in Mongolia, with the highest quality garments.
Machines are used to circular knit the sleeves, the legs, and the body for best possible fit and comfort.
The advantage of using modern technology is that garments can be worn directly against the skin with minimum seams, resulting in flexible and comfortable garments.
Once a garment is finished, its checked for highest quality finish and then it is packaged and distributed worldwide.
